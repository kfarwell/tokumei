Privacy Policy

  1. At this time and for the foreseeable future, this website is free to use in its entirety. There are no monetary costs associated with registering an account, creating a course or uploading any of your work.
  2. Sensitive user information like passwords are stored on our servers using a multi stage encryption process.
  3. User information is not being sold, traded, shared with or given to any third party(s).
  4. Our site does not track users' activity. Cookies from scholarly.me are used only to keep you logged in. The server does not store any access logs.
  5. We do not intend to make money from student work or put advertising of any sort on the website.
  6. We collect information like your university name and courses you're enrolled in for future personalization of the user experience.
  7. At this time, we do not send email notifications of any sort. If you receive one that claims to be from us, please let us know.
  8. You will be notified of any changes made to this privacy policy with a banner notification on the homepage of our website.
  9. Minors will need parental consent to use the site, as nudity and other explicit images might be depicted on the site in the form of paintings, sculptures and models.
