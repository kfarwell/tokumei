Privacy Policy

Information we collect

Post information: when you create a post on Tokumei, we record the following indefinitely:

  • The text that you post;
  • The terms that you tag your post with, if any;
  • The files that you upload with your post, if any;
  • The time and date that you submit your post.

Cookies: When you modify your preference for the number of posts to be displayed on each page of the post index, we set a cookie in your browser to keep track of your preference in the future. You can avoid this cookie being set by not modifying this setting with the form pictured below.

How we use this information

Post information: This information is made public in your posts on Tokumei. Anyone can see it. We (or anyone else) may analyze this public information that you submit for any purpose.

Cookies: We only read your browser's cookies to change the number of posts displayed for you per your preference.

Information we do not collect

Everything else! We do not store your IP address or any additional information about you for any amount of time.
