Privacy Policy

Information we collect

When you create a post on Tokumei, we record the following indefinitely:

  • The text that you post.
  • The terms that you tag your post with, if any.
  • The files that you upload with your post, if any.
  • The time and date that you submit your post.

How we use this information

The above information is made public in your posts on Tokumei. Anyone can see it. We (or anyone else) may analyze this public information that you submit for any purpose.

Information we do not collect

Everything else! We do not store your IP address or any additional information about you for any amount of time.
