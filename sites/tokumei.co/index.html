Anonymous

What you have to say is more important than who you are. You don't need to register an account before posting, and no one can link your posts back to you or together.

No, really

You're not just hidden from other users. Your connection to Tokumei is encrypted and your visit doesn't leave a trace on the server. We even have a censorship-resistant Tor hidden service and i2p eepsite.

Dead simple

No need to register, no complicated form to post. Just say what's on your mind. Even my dog managed to post on Tokumei once.

DEVELOPERS DEVELOPERS DEVELOPERS

Tokumei is free software released under the ISC license. Feel free to host your own Tokumei site, fork the code, or help contribute. You can find the git repo here. Tokumei is written in rc, the Plan 9 shell, using the werc minimal web framework. If you can write a shell script, you can extend Tokumei.

We've also got a simple plaintext API.

Tokumei is free and ad-free, but we're two broke high school students. Support free speech by contributing code or sending us a donation.
